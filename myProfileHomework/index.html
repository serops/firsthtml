About Me

Then I attended Haigazian University and graduated in 1999 with a degree in Economics. Then I got my Economics MA degree from Ottawa University in 2003.
Since 2000,I have worked as a partner with my two elder brothers managing a kitchen equipment company, which was established by my two brothersv in 1994. Currently I am living in Toronto, working part time for Rhine Kitchen: mainly doing project cost assesment, pricing, and technical supervisor.
